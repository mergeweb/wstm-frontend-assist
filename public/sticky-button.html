College is a journey. 

WestX helps you make the most of your experience. 

Build a college experience that's completely your own, from your first advising meeting to graduating with career confidence. 

 

 

Turn your time at Westminster into a world of opportunities. 

WestX is your guided, goal-orientated progress map that provides you with the team, the learning, and the milestones to explore your interests, choose your degree, advance your thinking, and turn your college experience into a fulfilling career.

Griffin Guides

Meet your support team. 

Begin your journey with the support of Westminster’s faculty, professional advisors, and fellow students behind you, providing counsel and guidance to help you adjust and thrive in this new experience.  

NAVIGATE YOUR JOURNEY

 

 

 

learning communities

What happens when you dive deeply into subjects and push the boundaries of your own thinking? Our Westminster’s Learning Communities help you expand your knowledge and uncover what you want to spend more time studying. 
 

FIND YOUR COMMUNITY

 

 

 

honors

What if your classes were designed around helping you practice the habit of thinking? Westminster's Honors College offers an exciting course of study for academically and intellectually prepared students like you. 
 

EXPLORE HONORS

 

 

 

WCORE

Every Westminster student completes WCore as the heart of their education. You’ll develop deeper critical thinking skills and lay the foundation for thoughtful exploration throughout the rest of your college experience. 
 

EXPLORE WCORE

 

 

 

pathways

Whether you already know your major, have a few ideas, or no clue what you want to do, Westminster’s professional advisors use Pathways to help you build a schedule that helps you explore your interests. 
 

choose your path

 

 

 

design your own program

Have multiple interests you want to explore? WestX offers the flexibility to build your own curriculum, allowing you to focus on your areas of interests while discovering potentially new paths and confirming where you want to go next. 
 

explore your options

 

Griffin ready

Everyone deserves a chance to learn from real-world experience. WestX Griffin Ready opportunities are paid work experiences and undergraduate research options that help you gain powerful hands-on knowledge while building connections with those in the business.  

EXPLORE CAREER PATHS

 

 

 

student showcase

Creating and sharing the tangible results of your work is an important part of what makes WestX a dynamic and exciting opportunity. This is your chance to get as creative as you want to be: showcase a project, enact a performance piece, or prepare a video that represents your work experience

EXPLORE STUDENT SHOWCASE

 

 

 

career confidence

Your time in WestX prepares you with the insight, direction, and purpose to set the foundation for your next steps in life. You’ll reach higher and dig deeper than you ever thought possible, ready to graduate from Westminster career confident and determined to succeed. 
 

build your confidence
